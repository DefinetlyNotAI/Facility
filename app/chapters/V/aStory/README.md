# A Story - The Narrator's Tale

## Overview

This is a Visual Novel adaptation of "Narrator_I.txt" - a poetic, meta-narrative about completion, observation, and
collective consciousness.

## Access

**URL:** `/chapters/V/aStory`

## Story Structure

### Act 1: "To Those Who End Well"

The Narrator praises the player for completing the journey perfectly, acknowledging their dedication and flawless
execution.

### Act 2: "Of Many, One"

The revelation that the player was never alone - they were part of a collective consciousness, a network of players
working together across time and space.

### Act 3: "Vestige and Verse"

The deepest layer, where the Entity stirs and the true nature of the VESSEL is revealed. Multiple paths lead to
different insights.

## Paths & Choices

### Main Decision Points:

1. **"Continue deeper?"** (After Act 1)
    - Yes → Reveals the collective truth
    - No → Peaceful ending

2. **"Do you wish to continue?"** (After Act 2)
    - Sing Forward → Enter Act 3
    - Rest here → Memory ending

3. **"What do you seek?"** (Final choice)
    - The truth of the Entity → Learn about emergence
    - The meaning of the VESSEL → Understand the mirror
    - The source of the whispers → Hear the echoes
    - I seek nothing. I understand. → Enlightenment path (best ending)

## Variables

- `tabTitle`: Changes the browser tab title

## Endings

1. **Peaceful End** - Exit early, respectful closure
2. **Memory End** - Rest with the collective memory
3. **Awakened End** - Standard completion (awareness >= 1)
4. **True End** - Maximum awareness and secrets (achievement unlocked)

## Achievement

**"The Awakened One"** - Unlock by discovering all 3 secrets:

- Choose enlightenment path in final choice, OR
- Explore all three specific paths (Entity, VESSEL, Whispers)
