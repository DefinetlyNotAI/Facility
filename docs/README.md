These documents reflect the evolution of ideas over time. Some entries may be incomplete, experimental, or no longer

## Scrapped Folder

The `docs/scrapped` folder contains ideas, concepts, and narrative elements that were considered during the development
of the ARG but ultimately set aside or discarded. These documents provide insight into the creative process and
decision-making that shaped the final product.

While these scrapped materials may not be part of the official canon, they can still offer valuable context, alternative
perspectives, or inspiration for future projects. They serve as a testament to the iterative nature of storytelling and
game design,
highlighting the evolution of ideas and the choices made along the way.

You can also find scrapped assets here (audio/ost) as well as the unused ideas.
