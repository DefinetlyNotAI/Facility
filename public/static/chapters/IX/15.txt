1. When no one is looking, who do you become?
2. Does life start when it’s seen, or before anyone knows it exists?
3. Do you choose, or are you chosen by the surrounding currents under the moon?
4. Memory slips, reshapes itself. How do you know you ever really lived?
5. Strip away your name, your face, your place in the world-what stays?

---

6. Pain shapes us. Without it, do we ever grow?
7. Change comes like a quiet storm-does it make you more yourself, or less?
8. When something fractures and carries on, is it surviving… or already dying?
9. Do you bend to survive, or survive by bending?
10. Growth can twist into something strange. How do you know when it’s too far?

---

11. If ending could come painless and fast, would you take it-or step aside?
12. Endless life stretches meaning thin, but perhaps it also reveals it. Which is true for you?
13. The dead linger, silently. Do they judge, or simply wait?
14. Is it worse to vanish, or to be remembered wrong?
15. When the final moment arrives, does it carry you forward-or leave only a shadow behind?
